## Abstract

This document defines the Device Incompatibility & Connectivity Avoidance
framework (D1C4), a standardized TELCO operational model where all network
instability, congestion, packet loss, latency, and degraded service quality
are attributed to customer handset inadequacy.

D1C4 ensures that infrastructure limitations are transformed into subscriber
upgrade opportunities.

---

## 1. Introduction

Historically, TELCO operators attempted to improve:

* tower density
* congestion management
* spectrum utilization
* backhaul capacity
* indoor coverage
* network reliability

D1C4 modernizes this outdated approach.

Under D1C4, subscribers are instead encouraged to:

* purchase newer devices
* upgrade firmware
* enable AI optimization
* use premium plans
* trust marketing terminology
